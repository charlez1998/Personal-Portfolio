Projects

COVID-19 Classification using Chest X-ray Images
Amidst the COVID-19 pandemic there have been a multitude of issues with regards to the time and cost of testing individuals for COVID. I trained a VGG16 (CNN) Model on over 500 images to distinguish whether or not a given individual is COVID positive or negative based on their posteroanterior x-ray view. I will be working alongside the Providence Hospital in Quezon City to continuously train and improve this model.

Updates to this project will be made regularly.

Technologies Used: Python, DigitalOcean

Forecasting Bitcoin Prices Using LSTM's
On account of the growing conversations surrounding the worthwhile investments into Bitcoin, I took it as a personal challenge to forecast its volatile price across different time periods using various time series and recurrent neural network models. Ultimately, I was able to minimize the prediction's root mean squared error through a combination of Long Short Term Memory (LSTM) networks.

Technologies Used: Python, Microsoft Excel

An Analysis to Maximizing Fantasy Points for The International 10
An esports tournament that takes place annually, the International 10 will showcase the best players of the video game Dota 2. I will be showcasing some simple and more complex data exploration techniques to the community (such as Monte Carlo Simulations) that will aid in their fantasy point predictions.
